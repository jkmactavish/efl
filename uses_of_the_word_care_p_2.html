Uses of the word care, p. 2

Task

Write three sentences, one using care for, one using care about, one using care of.

Send to your instructor to check if each is correct. Because:

  • He takes care of the lessons that will help his students learn.
  • He cares for the progress of all his students.
  • He cares about you. (Yikes?!)

To be clear about the meaning of Yikes in the last example, why might a student be especially surprised if an instructor said he cared about him or her?
